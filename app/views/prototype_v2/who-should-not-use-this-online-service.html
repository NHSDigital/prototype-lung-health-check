You need to do your NHS lung cancer risk check in person, not online, if you:
  • have been diagnosed with an eating disorder, or think you may have one
  • have a condition that affects your height
  • have had a limb amputation, or were born with a limb difference
  • are pregnant

This is because the online service uses automatic calculations based on your height and weight. It cannot give accurate results in these cases.
