You should not continue to use the NHS lung health check online if you:

You should not continue to use the NHS lung health check online if you:
  • have been diagnosed with an eating disorder, or think you may have one
  • ahave a condition that affects your height
  • have had a limb amputation, or were born with a limb difference
  • are pregnant

This is because the online service uses automatic calculations. It cannot give accurate results in these cases.
