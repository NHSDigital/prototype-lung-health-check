The NHS Lung Health Check is a free check-up of your health. It can help spot early signs of conditions like lung disease and cancer.

Doing your Lung Health Check online means you do not have to make a GP appointment. The results are sent to your GP.

You're eligible for this service when you:

  • are between 40 and 74 years old
  • are currently or have previously smoked
